About us




Who are we

We are a diverse team made up of neuroscientists, biomechanical engineers and entrepreneurs dedicated to providing this new, revolutionary and exciting opportunity to as many people as we can reach.


What do we do

Using our tested and patented technology, we facilitate the exchange of braincells between people who are willing to sell or lend their extra braincells and the people who would like to buy or rent said braincells.


Where can you find us

Given that we upload the braincells onto the cloud, you can find and use our services anywhere that has internet access and a computer.
